# 🩺 FindADoc

> **Find trusted doctors anywhere.**
>
> Making healthcare accessible for travelers through verified doctors, transparent pricing, and seamless appointment booking.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Imagine you're traveling to a new city and suddenly need medical assistance.

Finding a trustworthy doctor quickly can be difficult because:
- ❌ No single platform with verified doctors
- ❌ Hidden consultation costs
- ❌ Difficult appointment booking
- ❌ Limited information about doctors
- ❌ Language barriers
- ❌ Hard to compare available options

**FindADoc** solves these problems by providing a simple platform where travelers can discover, compare, and book appointments with trusted healthcare professionals.
